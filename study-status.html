Your study status

Tell us your plans

According to our records you are not currently studying. You can enrol on your next module(s) now. If you're not planning to study you need to let us know, tell us your intentions below.

Select the statement that matches your study plans

I plan to start my next module(s) at the next available start date

Choose next modules

.

The majority of our modules start in October and enrolment for these usually opens around February. So if you are looking to enrol between mid-September and February you may need to wait for enrolment to be open.

Please confirm:

I need some time off from study
You need to formally tell us if you're taking a break from studying your qualification. Find out more about requesting a study break.
I no longer want to study with the OU
If you tell us that you don't intend to study we won't send you information about choosing your next modules. You may be entitled to claim a qualification with the study you have already completed.
