Online Learning Environment preferences

You can customise some aspects of your study planner to suit your own preferences. Your settings are entirely private to you. They apply to all your module websites in whatever browser or device you use, although the preferred format option is not available on mobile devices.

Preferred format

The format that you select here will be available for download directly on your study planner and resource pages, and will be indicated by an icon alongside the web link. This preference will apply to all of your module websites. Please note that not all formats are available for each module; this depends on technical aspects of the materials. Select ‘Web’ to remove the preferred format icons from the study planner.
